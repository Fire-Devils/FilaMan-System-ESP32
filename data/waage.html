Calibration Procedure

Follow these steps carefully to ensure accuracy:

  1. Ensure the scale is completely empty.
  2. Prepare a 500g calibration weight.
  3. Click "Start Calibration" below.
  4. Step 1: Wait for zero-point reading.
  5. Step 2: Place the 500g weight on the scale when prompted.
  6. Step 3: Remove the weight.
